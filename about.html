my story

I'm Amelie Lucille — a 17-year-old singer-songwriter and guitarist studying at LaGuardia High School. I grew up in New York City, and the city is all over my music: the way I write, the sounds I gravitate toward, the stories I tell.

A lot of it traces back to my dad's record collection. Growing up inside his music taste shaped the way I hear songs, and it's a big part of why I started writing my own. Most of them start on guitar — a chord progression, a feeling, and wherever that goes.
